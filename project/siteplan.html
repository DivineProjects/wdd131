Site Plan

Site Name

Go Wild - The website promotive wildlife tourism, mental and physical well-being through visiting Zimbabwean game parks and natural landmarks.

Site Purpose

Go Wild helps visitors to discover and engage with local parks, trails, and reserves in Zimbabwe. The site also promotes mental and physical wellness through the exploration of Zimbabwe's game parks and nature reserves.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the most diverse wildlife i Zimbabwe
  • Where can I go hiking and camping near my town, that offers scenic views and peaceful surroundings?
  • What type of activities can I enjoy at the parks
  • How can visiting nature improve my mental health?

Color Scheme

color schemes are inspired by the colors of the savana grasslands

  • Primary: #4F6F52 (forest green, used for header or footer or or text)
  • Secondary: #19198f (blue, used for highlights and links)
  • Accent: #F5F5DC (light cream, used for backgrounds)

Typography

Font: Open Sans (used for body and headings for readability)

Fonts:

  • Open Sans: This will be the primary font used for the body text and headings throughout the site for improved readability.
  • Roboto: This will be used for buttons and special elements like call-to-action text, as it has a modern and clean look.
  • Playfair Display: This serif font will be used for titles and quotes in specific sections where a more elegant, sophisticated feel is needed.

All fonts are sourced from Google Fonts for easy accessibility and performance optimization.
